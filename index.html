How submitted information is used.

01

Information collected

Depending on the form, Ads Success PH may collect your name, email, contact details, business information, project requirements, campaign details, selected package, budget information, and customer review.

02

Purpose

Your information is used to review inquiries, evaluate project requirements, prepare for requested services, communicate with you, manage lead or project status, and moderate customer reviews.

03

Storage and access

The current system uses Google Sheets connected through Google Apps Script. The database spreadsheet should remain restricted and is intended for authorized Ads Success PH admin access only. Public sample and package images may be stored separately in a publicly viewable Google Drive folder.
